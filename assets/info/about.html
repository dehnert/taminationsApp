Welcome to Taminations

Here you will find animations for square dance calls from Mainstream through C3B. Most calls have several animations from different starting positions. Taminations are written and maintained by Brad Christie, webmaster of the Tam Twirlers Square Dance Club.

Taminations is not affiliated with CALLERLAB. For official and complete definitions, refer to the CALLERLAB documents. For Challenge definitions, the web site and books by Vic Ceder are another important reference.
